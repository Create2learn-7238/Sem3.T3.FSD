FSD most commonly refers to Tesla's Full Self-Driving capability, a supervised driver-assist system that handles steering, acceleration, and braking. It can navigate city streets, highways, and roundabouts but requires constant driver supervision to intervene when needed. Another meaning of FSD is a Flame Supervision Device, which cuts off gas to an appliance if the flame goes out.

Probability is the measure of the likelihood of an event occurring, dealing with chance and randomness. It is expressed as a number between 0 and 1, where 0 means the event is impossible and 1 means it is certain. Statistics is the branch of mathematics dealing with the collection, analysis, interpretation, presentation, and organization of data. The basic formula for probability is the number of favorable outcomes divided by the total number of possible outcomes.
